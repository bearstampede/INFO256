SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO RENEW ISRAEL'S WEOG MEMBERSHIP:  TURKEY 
 
 
REF: STATE 38273

International Political Organizations Department Head Murat 
Ates.  Ates noted Turkey's past support for Israel's WEOG 
membership and commented that Turkey usually favorably 
evaluates such requests.  Ates undertook to consult the MFA 
Middle East Department on reftel request.
