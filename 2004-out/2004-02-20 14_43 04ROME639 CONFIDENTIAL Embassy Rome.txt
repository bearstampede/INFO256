SUBJECT: UNWRA EMERGENCY APPEAL -- ITALY STRAPPED FOR FUNDS 
 
REF: STATE 29074 
 
CLASSIFIED BY: ACTING POL M/C GARY ROBBINS FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D). 
 
¶1. (C) POL M/C DELIVERED REFTEL DEMARCHE TO MFA OFFICE 
DIRECTOR FOR THE EASTERN MEDITERRANEAN LUCA FERRARI. FERRARI 
WAS NOT OPTIMISTIC THAT THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT COULD COME UP 
WITH MORE FUNDS TO SUPPORT THE UNWRA, GIVEN THAT IT IS 
    ALREADY STRAPPED TRYING TO SUPPORT NUMEROUS MISSIONS ABROAD, 
INCLUDING IRAQ.  COMMENT. ON FEBRUARY 18 THE ITALIAN SENATE 
APPROVED APPROXIMATELY USD SEVEN HUNDRED MILLION TO EXTEND 
ALL OF ITALY'S PEACEKEEPING AND HUMANITARIAN MISSIONS ABROAD 
THROUGH JUNE 30, 2004 (SEPTEL).  END COMMENT. 
 
¶2. (C) POLOFF ALSO DELIVERED THE DEMARCHE TO CARLO CAMPANILE 
IN THE MFA'S UN/HUMAN RIGHTS OFFICE.  CAMPANILE THANKED 
POLOFF FOR THE INFORMATION BUT SAID HIS OFFICE HAD NO 
INFORMATION ON FUNDING FOR UNWRA, ADDING, "YOUR COFFER IS 
BIGGER THAN OURS!"
