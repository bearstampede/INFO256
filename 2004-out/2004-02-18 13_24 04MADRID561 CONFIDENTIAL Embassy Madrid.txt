 ¶1. (C) Per reftel A, we spoke with Inigo de Palacio, MFA 
Director of UN Affairs, on February 9 to deliver demarche on 
voluntary contributions to the Special Court for Sierra 
Leone.  De Palacio said he could not give a response at that 
time, but would pursue the issue within the MFA.  On February 
17, he informed us that he did not foresee Spain being able 
to commit to additional funding until the MFA reviews its 
foreign aid program and budget at the end of March.
