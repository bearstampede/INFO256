SUBJECT:  REVISED TRADE AND INVESTMENT FRAMEWORK AGREEMENT 
TIFA) WITH THE UNITED ARAB EMIRATES 
 
REF: STATE 27312 
 
¶1. Post received a diplomatic note from the UAE MFA with 
the final text of the TIFA.  There were no changes to the 
text, from the USG revised text reftel.  The UAEG requests 
that we fix a signing date at the earliest opportunity. 
Post will fax the English language text to the Desk and to 
USTR.  We are expecting a copy of the Arabic text shortly 
and will pass it as soon as we receive it. 
 
¶2. Informal Embassy translation of the diplomatic note 
follows: 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the State of the United 
Arab Emirates directs its complements to the Embassy of the 
United States and has the honor to send the draft text of 
the Trade and Investment Framework Agreement (TIFA) between 
the United Arab Emirates and the United States in its final 
form.  Please propose the earliest possible time to sign 
the agreement. 
 
¶3. Action Request:  Please advise as to dates in March that 
we can propose to the UAEG for signature.
