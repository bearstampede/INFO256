¶2.  EMBASSY OFFICERS PRESENTED REFTEL POINTS TO OFFICIALS OF 
THE MINISTRIES OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS AND TRANSPORT, STRESSING 
THE NEED TO AMEND THE SUPPRESION OF UNLAWFUL ACTS AGAINST 
SAFETY OF MARITIME NAVIGATION (SUA) CONVENTION TO CRIMINALIZE 
WMD TRANSPORT. 
 
¶3. TRANSPORT MINISTRY DIRECTOR FOR MARITIME AFFAIRS, COSIMO 
CALIENDO, TOLD SCIENCE COUNSELOR FEBRUARY 17 THAT ITALY FULLY 
SUPPORTED THE GOAL OF THE U.S. EFFORT.  HOWEVER, HE ADDED, IT 
WAS NECESSARY TO FIND A WAY TO ACHIEVE THIS GOAL WITH THE 
LEAST NEGATIVE IMPACT ON THE MARITIME SECTOR.  EXPERTS FROM 
THE MINISTRIES OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS AND TRANSPORT WERE STILL 
STUDYING THE PROPOSAL AND ITS POTENTIAL IMPACT.
