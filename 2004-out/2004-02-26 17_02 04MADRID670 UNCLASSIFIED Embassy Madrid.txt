We sent reftel points to the MFA and to the two lead Spanish 
Economy Ministry delegates to the February 28-29 IRFFI 
meeting in Abu Dhabi; Ramon Guzman, Director General for 
International Finance, and Carlos Jimenez, Director General 
for Commercial Policy.  Guzman and Jimenez were preparing to 
leave this afternoon for London and then Abu Dhabi.  While we 
were unable to speak with them, Embassy confirmed that our 
message was delivered to both officials before they departed.
