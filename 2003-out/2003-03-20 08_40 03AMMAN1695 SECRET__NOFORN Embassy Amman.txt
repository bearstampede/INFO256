SUBJECT: TFIZ01:  JORDAN NOT INTERESTED IN BEING PROTECTING 
POWER FOR IRAQ 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 72921 
 
     ¶B. AMMAN 1686 
 
Classified By: A/DCM Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D) 
 
¶1.  (S/NF)  In his March 19 conversation with King Abdullah 
and GID Director Saad Kheir, the Ambassador conveyed USG 
concerns regarding the presence of Iraqi personnel in Jordan, 
and the King promised to take all necessary actions to 
protect American and Jordanian interests from a possible 
Iraqi threat (ref b). 
 
¶2.  (S)  A/DCM on March 20 conveyed to Ali al-Ayed, Personal 
Assistant to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher, ref a question 
regarding the willingness of Jordan to provide protecting 
consular services to Iraqi nationals.  Al-Ayed responded 
immediately that Jordan would not/not be interested in 
providing such services.
