SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO GOT ON US CANDIDATE FOR COMMITTEE 
AGAINST TORTURE 
 
 
REF: SECSTATE 287167 
 
 
Poloff October 14 delivered reftel dipnote and talking points 
to Ali Savut, head of the MFA Human Rights Department.  Savut 
replied October 15 that GOT officials in Ankara and Geneva 
are reviewing Gaer and the other candidates for the UN 
Committee Against Torture.  He said the GOT has not yet 
determined which candidates it will support at the November 
26 Convention Against Torture.
