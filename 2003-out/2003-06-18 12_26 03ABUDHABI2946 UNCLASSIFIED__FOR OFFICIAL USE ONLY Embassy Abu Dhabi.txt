SUBJECT:  UAE ATTENDANCE AT WEF 6/23 BREAKFAST 
 
REF:  STATE 161582 
 
¶1.  (U) This message contains an action request; 
please see paragraph 3. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Post delivered 6/14 the Secretary's message 
regarding the WEF working breakfast (see ref) to de 
facto UAE Foreign Minister Hamdan bin Zayid.  Hamdan's 
Office Director informed Polchief 6/17 that Dubai 
Crown Prince (and de facto ruler) Shaykh Muhammad bin 
Rashid Al-Maktoum (MbR) would lead the UAE delegation 
to WEF in Amman.  MbR's office subsequently confirmed 
to CG Dubai that he would attend the breakfast.  The 
MFA separately informed us that de facto Finance 
Minister Dr. Muhammad Khalfan bin Khirbash would also 
be available to attend the breakfast. 
 
¶3.  (U) Action request: Post has not received any 
information regarding the venue or time of the 6/23 
breakfast, and would appreciate Department passing 
this information to us or to the UAE Embassy in Amman. 
End action request. 
 
¶4.  (U) This message has been coordinated with Congen 
Dubai.
