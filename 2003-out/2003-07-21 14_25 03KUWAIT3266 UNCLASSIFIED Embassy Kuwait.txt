SUBJECT: KUWAIT INVITED TO DESIGNATE CIC REPRESENTATIVE 
 
REF: STATE 206458 
 
¶1.  Post conveyed Reftel note verbale to Kuwait MFA on 19 
July inviting the GOK to designate a representative for 
appointment to the Council for International Cooperation 
(CIC) in Iraq.  MFA confirmed 21 July that U/S Amb Khaled 
Al-Jarallah has the request under consideration.  We 
anticipate a response by 22 July, expect that Kuwait will 
actively participate in CIC membership, and will report the 
official response when received. 
 
¶2.  Minimize considered.
