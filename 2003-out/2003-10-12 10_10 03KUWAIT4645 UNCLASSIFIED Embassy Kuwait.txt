SUBJECT: KUWAIT DEMARCHE DELIVERED: MADRID CONFERENCE ON 
THE ROLE OF THE PRIVATE SECTOR IN IRAQ 
 
REF: SECSTATE 287995 
 
Embassy confirmed with the Kuwaiti Ministry of Finance that 
they have received the Spanish letter on the Madrid "Role of 
the Private Sector in Development of the New Iraq" 
conference.  The Ministry has forwarded the letter to the 
Kuwait Chamber of Commerce, which is providing the 
information to its members.  The Chamber of Commerce will 
take the lead in organizing the delegation, and will contact 
the Spanish directly with the names of the Kuwaiti 
delegation.  Post will relay any additional information that 
we receive.
