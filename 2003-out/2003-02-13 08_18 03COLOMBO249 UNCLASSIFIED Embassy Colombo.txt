¶A.   A.   DETAILED PRODUCT DESCRIPTION: Expressions of 
     interest has been called from companies with experience 
     in coastal and off-shore exploration and exploitation of 
     mineral deposits, to exploit 170 million cubic meters of 
     heavy mineral deposits in the top 2 meters of the seabed. 
     The mineral deposits consist of Monazite, Ilmenite, 
     Rutile and Zircon.
