SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE: JORDAN: AL-ZARQAWI (LIST 41) 
 
REF: SECSTATE 270667 
 
Classified By: Acting DCM Doug Silliman.  Reasons 1.5 (b) and (d) 
 
¶1.  (C)  The Jordanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs replied by 
classified diplomatic note to the Embassy's note of September 
25 regarding the co-sponsorship by the United States and 
Germany of Abu Musa'ab al-Zarqawi to be added to the United 
Nations lists.  Following is the Embassy's translation of the 
Jordanian note. 
 
¶2.  (C)  Begin text. 
 
(Complimentary opening)  With reference to the Embassy's note 
number 1519 dated September 25, 2003 containing the Embassy's 
request to investigate the presence of offices or other 
financial assets of terrorist-linked organizations in Jordan 
and welcoming any information about the subject cell (note: 
the al-Zarqawi group), the Ministry has the honor to inform 
the Embassy of Jordanian efforts in this regard. 
 
-  The relevant Jordanian authorities discovered the true 
names and nationalities of the cell resident in Germany 
associated with "Ahmad Fadhel al-Khalailah," alias "Abu 
Musa'ab al-Zarqawi," and discovered the activities of its 
members and the locations of their military training. 
 
-  The judicial authorities in Jordan provided the judicial 
authorities in Germany with the available information 
regarding the cell's personnel arrested in Germany. 
 
(Complimentary closing) 
End Text.
