SUBJECT:  ICC:  Non-surrender agreement with the

¶1.  (U) This message is Sensitive but Unclassified -- 
Please handle accordingly. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) The Maldivian government has sent Mission a 
dipnote confirming that it has completed the necessary 
domestic legal requirements for the entry into force of 
the non-surrender agreement it concluded with the U.S. 
in April 2003.  The Maldivian government considers the 
agreement's entry into force to be July 8, 2003 (the 
date of its dipnote).  A copy of the dipnote is 
contained in Ref A.
