SUBJECT: UAEG INFORMS POST OF DELEGATION TO THE WTO 
MINISTERIAL IN CANCUN 
 
REF: EMAIL FROM J. BUNTIN (08/28/03) 
 
¶1.  AT THE REQUEST OF POST, THE UAE MINISTRY OF ECONOMY 
PROVIDED A LIST OF UAE DELEGATES TO ATTEND THE WTO 
MINISTERIAL IN CANCUN, SEPTEMBER 8-14.  UAE MINISTER OF 
ECONOMY AND COMMERCE SHEIKH FAHIM BIN SULTAN AL-QASSIMI WILL 
HEAD THE UAE DELEGATION TO CANCUN, WHICH WILL ALSO INCLUDE 
THE SECRETARY GENERAL OF THE FEDERATION OF UAE CHAMBERS OF 
COMMERCE AND REPRESENTATIVES FROM THE MINISTRIES OF ECONOMY, 
FINANCE, JUSTICE, AGRICULTURE, LABOR, PETROLEUM, 
INFORMATION, THE UAE CENTRAL BANK, AND THE UAE'S MONOPOLY 
TELECOM PROVIDER, ETISALAT.  POST WILL FAX THE COMPLETE 
DELEGATION LIST TO THE DEPARTMENT AND USTR. 
 
¶2.  UAE MINISTRY OF ECONOMY, WTO AFFAIRS OFFICE DIRECTOR 
SAEED AL-NASIBI IS THE COORDINATOR FOR THE UAE DELEGATION'S 
SCHEDULE WHILE IN CANCUN.  AL-NASIBI TOLD ECONOFF THAT THE 
DELEGATES' SCHEDULES ARE VERY BUSY THAT WEEK, BUT HE WOULD 
BE AMENABLE TO TRYING TO ARRANGE A MEETING WITH U.S. 
OFFICIALS ON THE SIDELINES OF THE MINISTERIAL.  AL-NASIBI 
CAN BE REACHED IN CANCUN AT THE HILTON CANCUN BEACH AND GOLF 
RESORT, PHONE +52 998 881 80 00 AND FAX +52 998 881 80 80.
