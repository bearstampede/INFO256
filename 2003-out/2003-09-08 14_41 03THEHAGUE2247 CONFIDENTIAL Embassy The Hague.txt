¶1. (U) This is an action request, see para 4. 
 
¶2. (C) Dutch Minister of Defense Henk Kamp plans to travel to 
Iraq from September 24-25 to visit UK Division Headquarters 
in Al Basra and meet with Dutch troops participating in 
stabilization operations in Al Muthanna on September 24.  The 
Minister requests a meeting with Ambassador Bremer in Baghdad 
on the morning of September 25. 
 
¶3. (C) Per ref A, the GONL requests security escort for the 
visit of the Minister while in Baghdad.  We understand per 
ref B that the Dutch Embassy in Washington is making this 
request via diplomatic note to the Department of State. 
 
¶4. (C) Action request:  Mission seeks Department assistance 
in securing an appointment for Minister Kamp on September 25 
with Amb. Bremer.
