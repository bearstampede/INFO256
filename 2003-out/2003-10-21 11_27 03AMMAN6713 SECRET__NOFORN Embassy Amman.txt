SUBJECT: JORDAN-IRAQ:  SECURE DISPOSITION OF RADIOACTIVE 
SOURCES FROM IRAQ 
 
REF: SECSTATE 293401 
 
Classified By: DCM David M. Hale for reasons 1.5 (B)(D) 
 
¶1.  (C)  PolCouns on October 20 conveyed to Ali al-Ayed, 
Director of Foreign Minister Muasher's Private Office, and 
Basheer Zoubi, MFA Private Office Iraq Action Officer, ref 
points on the need to identify and secure orphaned and 
illicitly trafficked radioactive sources from Iraq.  Ayed 
said he understood that Jordanian border inspectors are 
already under instructions to identify and seize suspicious 
shipments, vehicles, and persons coming from Iraq, including 
shipments with excessive radioactivity.  He promised to pass 
the points to the relevant GOJ agencies. 
 
¶2.  (S/NF)  ORCA will also double track these points in its 
channels.
