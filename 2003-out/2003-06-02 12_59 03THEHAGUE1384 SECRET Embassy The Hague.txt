(S) Acting PolCouns delivered ref A points to MFA Security 
Policy Head Maurits Jochems on May 30 and followed up with 
MFA Nonproliferation Office Director Paul Wilke on June 2. 
They appreciated the advance notice of the President's 
remarks, and noted Dutch MFA PolDir Hugo Siblesz's view that 
including France and Germany is important to the success of 
this effort.  The Dutch will participate in the June 12 
meeting.  Wilke said the issues identified in the nonpaper 
(ref B) are "clear," but was interested in additional details 
as to the USG's desired results from the Madrid meeting.
