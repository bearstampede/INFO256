SUBJECT: RESPONSE TO INTERNATIONAL MARITIME ORGANIZATION - 
DEMARCHE IN SUPPORT OF US CANDIDACY 
 
REF: SECSTATE 281005 
 
Per diplomatic note dated October 21, the GOJ has indicated 
its support for US membership in the IMO Council.  The same 
note asked for  U.S. support for Jordanian candidates for the 
International Committee of the Red Cross and Red Crescent, 
which Embassy will report by septel.
