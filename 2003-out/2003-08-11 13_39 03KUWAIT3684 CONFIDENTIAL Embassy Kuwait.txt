SUBJECT: (C) SHAYKH SABAH ACCEPTS POTUS' INVITATION 
 
Classified By: (U) CDA FRANK URBANCIC; REASON: 1.5 (B,D) 
 
¶1.  (C) Post has received a dipnote dated August 11 accepting 
President Bush's invitation to meet in the Oval Office at 
11:25 a.m. on Wednesday, September 10, followed by a media 
event then lunch in the Old Family Dining Room.  The GOK is 
also accepting the US offer to arrange a schedule of meetings 
with appropriate Cabinet Secretaries and agency heads.  We 
propose the Secretaries of State, Defense, Treasury, and 
Commerce, and DCI. 
 
¶2.  (C) We are still trying to clarify whether the GOK wishes 
us to request Blair House, and will advise soonest. 
URBANCIC
