SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED:  OPPOSING CUBA'S UN RESOLUTION 
AGAINST THE EMBARGO. 
 
REF: SECSTATE 301451 
 
DCM delivered reftel talking points on October 28 to 
Ambassador Khaled Al-Babtain, Director of the Americas 
Department at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and asked that 
Kuwait either vote against or abstain from voting on the 
Cuban resolution.  Al-Babtain commented that Kuwait generally 
votes yes on the resolution, but that he would check to see 
how Kuwait would vote this year.  Post will report any 
further response separately.
