SUBJECT:  ICC ARTICLE 98:  TEXT DELIVERED TO UAE 
 
¶1. (U) Classified by Richard A. Albright, Charge 
d'Affaires, a.i. for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D). 
 
¶2. (C) Charge delivered the text of the ICC Article 
98 Agreement to MFA Minstate Sheikh Hamdan bin 
Zayed Al-Nahyan's Office Director, Sultan Al- 
Romeithi, on August 17.  Charge urged prompt 
follow-up, noting the principal issues had been 
clarified in the July video conference and that 
there was high-level U.S. interest in finalizing 
this agreement quickly.  Al-Romeithi informed 
Polchief August 19 that the Foreign Minister had 
sent a letter to the Justice Minister urging him to 
intensify work on finalizing the agreement. 
 
¶3. (U) Deputy Legal Affairs Director Yacoub Al- 
Hosani, who has been our primary MFA interlocutor 
on ICC Article 98, has returned from leave and we 
will continue to follow up with the MFA.
