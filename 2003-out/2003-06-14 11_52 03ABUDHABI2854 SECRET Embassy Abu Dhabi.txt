SUBJECT:  (S) UAE ASKS IF FORMER IRAQI INFO MINISTER 
CAN TRAVEL HERE 
 
¶1.  (U)  CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR MARCELLE M. WAHBA 
FOR REASONS 1.5 (B, D). 
 
 
¶2.  (U)  This is an action message.  Please see 
paragraph four. 
 
¶3.  (S)  MFA Minstate Shaykh Hamdan bin Zayid (HBZ) 
contacted the Ambassador 6/13 to say that the UAEG had 
received a request from former Iraqi Information 
Minister Al-Sahhaf to travel to the UAE.  Shaykh 
Hamdan said that before responding to Al-Sahhaf, the 
UAEG wanted to know if there were any USG 
sensitivities regarding Al-Sahhaf's traveling here. 
HBZ did not state what the purpose of Al-Sahhaf's 
travel was or the intended duration of his stay in the 
UAE.  We note that former Iraqi Ambassador to the UN 
Muhammad Al-Douri now works for Dubai-based, pan-Arab 
satellite channel Al-Arabiyya. 
 
¶4.  (U)  Action Request:  Department please advise how 
we may respond to the UAEG inquiry. 
 
¶5.  (U)  Baghdad minimize considered. 
 
Wahba
