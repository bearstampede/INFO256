SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO PA: JORDAN 
 
REF: STATE 348512 
 
Classified By: Ambassador Edward W. Gnehm for Reasons 1.5 (b),(d) 
 
 (C) Acting PolCouns presented December 31 ref talking points 
to Ali al-Ayed, Director of Foreign Minister Muasher's 
Private Office.  Ayed discussed the matter with FM Muasher 
and reported that as a recipient of international aid, Jordan 
is unable to offer any budgetary assistance to the 
Palestinian Authority to enable it to continue functioning 
through the end of the year.  Ayed also stated that Jordan 
does not intend to meet its Arab League financial obligations 
towards the Palestinians, a position that post has heard from 
the GOJ before.
