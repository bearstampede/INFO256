NBC Kuwait informed post early this morning of the death 
of NBC reporter David Bloom in Iraq today of natural 
causes (cerebral hemorrhage).  Mr. Bloom was embedded 
with the U.S. Army's 3rd Infantry Division in Iraq.  NBC 
notified NOK of Mr. Bloom's death.  The U.S. Army's 
Mortuary Affairs office in Kuwait is making arrangements 
for the immediate repatriation of Mr. Bloom's remains to 
the United States.  According to a U.S. Army Mortuary 
Affairs officer, Mr. Bloom's remains will be repatriated 
to Dover via military transport either tonight (April 6) 
or tomorrow (April 7) as soon as flight arrangements can 
be confirmed.  Mr. Bloom's remains are now in Kuwait in 
U.S. Army custody.  An autopsy will be performed upon 
arrival in Dover.
