SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE: JUDGE UPHOLDS DUTCH FREEZE OF AL 
AQSA FOUNDATION ASSETS 
 
REF: The Hague 1169 
 
(SBU) A Dutch judge ruled today (6/3) that the GONL had made 
its decision to freeze Al Aqsa Foundation assets on proper 
legal and factual basis.  Consequently, the Dutch will 
submit Al Aqsa Foundation to the EU Clearinghouse tomorrow 
(6/4) with a request that its assets be frozen European 
wide. Germany and the UK are expected to support the Dutch 
submission.
