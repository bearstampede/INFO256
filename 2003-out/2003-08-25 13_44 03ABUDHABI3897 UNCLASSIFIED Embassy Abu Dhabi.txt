SUBJECT:  POST INFORMS UAEG OF CONFERENCE ON INTELLECTUAL 
PROPERTY AND THE ECONOMICS OF INTERNATIONAL PUBLIC HEALTH 
 
REF: STATE 240113 
 
¶1.  Charge d'Affaires on 8/25 informed the UAE Minister of 
Health, de facto Minister of Economy, and Minister of 
Finance and Industry about the October conference on 
intellectual property and international public health in 
Washington, DC (reftel).  (Note: Responsibility for 
intellectual property rights as they relate to 
pharmaceuticals is divided in the UAEG among the Ministry 
of Economy WTO Affairs Office, Ministry of Finance 
Industrial Property Office, and Ministry of Health Drug 
Control Office.  End note). 
 
¶2.  Econoff separately passed ref information to the 
relevant office directors within each ministry and 
encouraged their participation in the conference.  Post 
will report any enquiries we receive regarding the 
conference.
