SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON POSSIBLE AL-QAIDA AVIATION THREAT 
 
REF: STATE 258046 
 
 
Classified by Economic Counselor Scot Marciel, reasons 1.5 B 
and D. 
 
 
(S) Econoff delivered reftel demarche on a possible Al-Qaida 
aviation threat to the United States to Ayten Eler, of the 
MFA Aviation Department, on September 9, and to Oktay Erdagi, 
Head of Aviation Security at the Civil Aviation General 
Directorate, on September 10.  Both took note of the threat 
information and promised to share it with appropriate 
officials involved in aviation security.  Erdagi confirmed 
that there are no direct scheduled flights connecting Turkey 
with destinations in the Western Hemisphere other than the 
United States.
