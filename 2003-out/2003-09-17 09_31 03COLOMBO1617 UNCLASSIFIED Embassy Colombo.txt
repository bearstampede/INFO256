SUBJECT:  Colombo Plan:  Drug Advisory Program proposed 
projects and funding for FY 2003 
 
 
¶1.  The Colombo Plan's Drug Advisory Program (DAP) 
submitted to Mission a budget proposal of USD 250,000 
for fiscal year 2003.  DAP's proposed budget includes 
funding for the following projects: 
 
¶A.  Training for South Asian Law Enforcement Officers on 
Counter-narcotics, USD 110,000 
 
¶B.  Regional Seminar/Workshop on the Role of Faith-Based 
Organizations and Spirituality in Drug Demand Reduction, 
USD 65,000 
 
¶C.  Asian Cities Against Drugs, USD 75,000. 
 
¶2.  Mission forwarded, via unclassified pouch, the 
proposal packet to INL/C/CJ.
