SUBJECT: DUTCH ON SUPPORTING THE UN'S REQUEST FOR LIBERIA 
 
REF: STATE 260879 
 
(SBU) As instructed reftel, PolMilOff conveyed U.S. support 
for the UN request for a Dutch Amphibious Transport Dock 
(LPD) and signals intelligence unit to MFA Security Policy 
Bureau Operations Chief Rob Gabrielse on September 12. 
Gabrielse said the issue of what assets the GONL might be 
able to supply for operations in Liberia was currently the 
subject of high-level discussion between Dutch MFA and MOD. 
MOD is studying what assets it might be able to make 
available, but no conclusions had been reached to date.
