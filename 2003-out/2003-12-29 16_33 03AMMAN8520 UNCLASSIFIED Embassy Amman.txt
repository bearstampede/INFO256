SUBJECT: Request for a DVC Program in Honor of Black History 
Month. 
 
 
¶1. Summary: In honor of Black History Month, post would like 
to request IIP's support in organizing a 90 minute DVC 
program discussing the influence of African Americans in 
Popular American Music. 
 
¶2. We expect an audience of 60 - 70 people for this DVC 
program.  The audience will consist of students from 
Jordanian high schools and musicians. 
 
¶3. Our recommendation of a theme and timing for the program 
is as follows: 
 
-    Hip-Hop Culture - A discussion featuring the evolution 
     of Hip-Hop in America: Wednesday, February 18, 1500 GMT. 
 
¶4. The control officer for this program will be CAO Donna 
Winton, Tel: 962-6-592-0101, Ext. 2115; Fax: 962-6-592-0121.
