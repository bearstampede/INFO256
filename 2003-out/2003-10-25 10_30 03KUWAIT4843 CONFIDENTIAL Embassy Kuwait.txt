SUBJECT: PALESTINIAN DEMARCHE DELIVERED TO MFA U/S 
AL-JARALLAH 
 
REF: SECSTATE 296490 
 
Classified By: Ambassador Richard H. Jones, per 1.5 (b), (d) 
 
(C) Ambassador delivered reftel demarche on the Palestinians 
to MFA Undersecretary Khaled Al-Jarallah on October 22. 
Ambassador provided reftel talking points to Al-Jarallah as a 
non-paper for MFA reference.  Al-Jarallah acknowledged that 
the situation in the West Bank and Gaza is deteriorating. He 
added that both the Israelis and the Palestinians are 
pursuing courses of action that threaten progress towards a 
viable solution.  Al-Jarallah said he hoped for some positive 
outcome in the Middle East soon and looked to upcoming 
negotiations to resolve the long-running conflict in the 
Sudan as a possible bright spot for a region sorely in need 
of good news.  Al-Jarallah accepted the non-paper on the USG 
position regarding the Palestinian Authority and promised to 
review it.
