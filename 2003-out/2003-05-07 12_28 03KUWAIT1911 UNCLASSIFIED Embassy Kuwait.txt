During a May 3 meeting with Kuwait's Deputy Prime Minister 
and Interior Minister Shaykh Mohammed Al-Sabah, the 
Ambassador shared the 2003 Special 301 public announcement 
(Ref A) and urged the Minister to intensify efforts to 
improve intellectual property rights protection and 
enforcement in Kuwait (Ref B).  Post also delivered the 
Special 301 excerpt to Shaykh Ahmed Al-Sabah, Kuwait's 
Minister of Information, and the complete text of the report 
to Shaykha Rasha Al-Sabah, Director of the Intellectual 
Property Department at the Ministry of Information.  Post 
plans to use the release of the Special 301 report to make a 
new push for IPR reform in Kuwait later this month.
