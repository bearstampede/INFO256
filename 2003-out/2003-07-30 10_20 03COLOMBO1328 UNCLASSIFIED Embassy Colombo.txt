SUBJECT:  Colombo Plan:  Amendment signed for 
USD 420,000 for demand reduction programs 
 
Refs:  (A) State 217238; (B) Colombo 1233 
 
Per Ref (A), Charge Entwistle and Secretary General of 
the Colombo Plan Kittipan Kanjanapipatkul signed an 
amendment to the Colombo Plan funding agreement for the 
allotment of USD 420,000 to the Drug Advisory Program on 
July 30.  The amendment provides funding for drug abuse 
and demand reduction programs (detailed in Ref B).  The 
signed amendment has been forwarded to INL/RM by 
registered pouch number 8026428.
