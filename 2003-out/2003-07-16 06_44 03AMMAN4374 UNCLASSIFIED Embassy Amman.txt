SUBJECT:  New Book Published  CAIRO PLEASE PASS TO ALEXANDRIA 
 
 
¶1.   Amman is pleased to announce a new title from the 
Arabic Book Program. "The Best American Short Stories of the 
Century", edited by John Updike and Katrina Kenison was 
published in early July. This book is the sixty-second in an 
annual series by the American publisher. This edition 
includes one hundred stories of which ninety-nine were 
translated and published in the Arabic edition. Translation 
rights for one story could not be obtained. The book 
contains 1008 pages, size 17 x 24 cms., hard cover. 
¶2.   Two sample copies are being dispatched to posts for 
evaluation. 
 
¶3.   Posts can place their orders, which will be sent on 
first-come-first-served basis. Quantities available are 
fairly limited. The book will be distributed by its 
Jordanian publisher throughout the Middle East and North 
Africa. Books including shipping, ordered directly by posts 
for distribution to contacts, are free of charge.
