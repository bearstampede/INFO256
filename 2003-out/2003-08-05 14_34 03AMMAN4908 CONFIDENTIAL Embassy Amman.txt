SUBJECT: SECRETARY'S ORAL MESSAGE TO ARAB LEAGUE FOREIGN 
MINISTERS 
 
REF: STATE 224109 
 
Classified By: Ambassador Edward W. Gnehm for Reasons 1.5 (B,D) 
 
¶1. (C) Ambassador delivered reftel oral message via telcon on 
August 4 to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher, who was in 
Kuwait on official business.  Ambassador stressed the need 
for Jordanian help in steering the Arab League to support the 
Iraqi Governing Council and Palestinian PM Abbas.  Muasher 
expressed his agreement with the Secretary's message and said 
that Jordan would do its part to try and achieve a positive 
outcome in Cairo. 
 
¶2. (C) PM Abbas enjoys close relations with the GOJ, as 
evidenced by his stops in Amman to meet with King Abdullah 
both before and after his recent trip to Washington.  During 
the latter, Abbas expressed optimism following his Washington 
meetings, according to FM Muasher.
