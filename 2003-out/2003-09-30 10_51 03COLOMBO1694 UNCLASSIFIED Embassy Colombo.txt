11.Comments: 
 
 
1.The Government of Sri Lanka (GOSL) though a special 
purpose project is calling for applications for pre- 
qualification from interested parties to finance, 
procure, install, commission, operate and maintain 
electronic cargo scanning services for container/ 
or container/ 
truck/cargo at the Port of Colombo and the International 
Airport for a period of 10 years. 
 
2.Following an operational period of ten (10) years, 
the Project Company will, at the option of GOSL, transfer 
ownership and all rights of the system to GOSL. 
 
3.Interested parties with or without domestic 
participation are eligible to submit application for pre- 
qualification.  Parties could be companies, corporations, 
firms, joint ventures, consortiums or other entities. 
4.Pre-qualification documents could be obtained during 
normal office hours, on payment of a non-refundable fee 
of Sri Lanka Rupees 25,000/- (US Dollar = SLR 94/-) from 
the Director General of Customs at the above address. 
Application for pre-qualification must be submitted on or 
before 14: 00 hours, October 10, 2003.
