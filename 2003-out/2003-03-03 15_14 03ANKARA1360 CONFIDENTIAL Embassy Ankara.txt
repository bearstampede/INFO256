SUBJECT: NORTHERN IRAQ: "HUNDREDS OF THOUSANDS" DEMONSTRATE 
AGAINST TURKISH INTERVENTION IN IRBIL, TURKISH FLAGS BURNED 
 
Classified by DCM Robert Deutsch.  Reasons 1.5 b and d. 
 
 
(C) Patriotic Union of Kurdistan (PUK) Ankara rep Bahros 
Galali called Operation Northern Watch POLAD March 3 to 
report that in Irbil, northern Iraq, approximately "300,000 
people" had marched from the regional parliament building to 
the UN compound in a peaceful demonstration earlier in the 
day protesting the possibility of Turkish military 
intervention in northern Iraq.  Galali said that some 
participants burned Turkish flags and that the GOT was 
already complaining about the activity.  We will try to learn 
more and report as more information becomes available.
