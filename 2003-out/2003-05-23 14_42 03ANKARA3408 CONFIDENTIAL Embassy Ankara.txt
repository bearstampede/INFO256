SUBJECT: GOT REQUESTS INFORMATION ON GUANTANAMO DETAINEES 
 
 
REF: A. ANKARA 796 
     ¶B. ISTANBUL 118 
 
 
Classified by A/DCM John Kunstadter; reasons 1.5 b and d. 
 
 
¶1. (U) This is an action request. 
 
 
¶2. (C) In a March 22 meeting with Ambassador, Interior 
Minister Aksu requested information on six Turkish citizens 
he said are being held at Guantanamo Bay Naval Base.  Aksu 
provided names, DOBs and POBs for the detainees, as follows: 
 
 
-- Murat Kurnaz; 3/19/82; Bremen, Germany (Embassy and 
Consulate Istanbul initially requested information on Kurnaz 
reftels). 
 
 
-- Salih Uyar; 4/14/81; Kocaeli, Turkey. 
 
 
-- Yuksel Celikgogus; 9/10/67; Karasu (Sakarya Province), 
Turkey. 
 
 
-- Ibrahim Sen; 10/1/80; Van, Turkey. 
 
 
-- Nuri Mert; 9/27/71; Dogubayazit (Agri Province), Turkey. 
 
 
-- Mesut Sen; 2/20/80; Brussels, Belgium. 
 
 
¶3. (C) Embassy requests guidance on how to respond to the 
inquiry, including any available information on the health 
and legal status of the detainees.
