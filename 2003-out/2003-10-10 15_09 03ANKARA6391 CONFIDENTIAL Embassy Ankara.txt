(C) DG for Bilateral Political Affairs Apakan told DCM 
October 9 that FM Gul and Greek FM Papandreou had agreed to 
cancel the respective Turkish-Cypriot and Greek-Cypriot 
military exercises that were scheduled to take place on 
Cyprus later in the fall.  Apakan said the agreement came 
during a meeting on the margins of a Mediterranean Forum 
meeting in Antalya earlier that day (October 9).
