SUBJECT: NEPALI GOVERNMENT DECLARES MAOISTS TERRORISTS 
 
REF: KATHMANDU 1648 
 
As expected (Reftel), on August 28 the Government of Nepal 
(GON) declared the Maoists and organizations affiliated with 
them terrorists.  The announcement was issued at 1800 local 
time.  The GON had lifted the terrorist designation shortly 
after a ceasefire was announced on January 29.
