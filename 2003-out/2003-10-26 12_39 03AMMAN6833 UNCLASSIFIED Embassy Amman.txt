SUBJECT: INTERNATIONAL VISITOR ALUMNUS HEADS JORDAN'S NEW 
GOVERNMENT

A NEW CABINET, WAS SWORN IN ON OCTOBER 25.  MR. AL FAYEZ IS 
AN ALUMNUS OF THE INTERNATIONAL VISITOR PROGRAM ON "ELECTION 
CAMPAIGN" IN 1988.

EXCHANGE PROGRAMS:

AFFAIRS.  DR. HILAYEL PARTICIPATED IN AN INDIVIDUAL IV

HABASHENEH PARTICIPATED IN A PARLIAMENTARY EXCHANGE PROGRAM
