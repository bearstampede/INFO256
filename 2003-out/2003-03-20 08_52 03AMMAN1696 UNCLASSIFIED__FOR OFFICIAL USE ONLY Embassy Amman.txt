¶2. (SBU) Embassy has gone to standfast.  Only a skeleton 
staff is at the Embassy and non-emergency American staff have 
been instructed to remain in their homes.  The Embassy 
remains open for emergency American Citizen Services, but has 
suspended visa and other routine public services for March 
20, 2003.  Current plans are to remain at standfast for 48 
hours after which we will assess appropriate staffing levels 
based on the local security environment.
