SUBJECT:  SECURITY EVALUATION OF REFUGEE PROCESSING SITE -

REF: A) HILL/POLASCHIK 10/14 E-MAIL 
 
     B) AMMAN 6514

As requested ref a, post confirms that prescreening and 
resettlement interviews for the 77 Sudanese and Somali 
refugees from UNHCR's Ruweished refugee camp will take place 
at the UNHCR office in Amman, Jordan.  UNHCR has agreed to 
bus the refugees from the Ruweished refugee camp to Amman 
and to provide hotel accommodations during the interviews. 
The GOJ also has agreed to these arrangements and will 
provide police escort for the refugees en route to and from 
Amman, as well as on-site security at the UNHCR office in 
Amman.  There will be no need for Embassy Amman or DHS staff 
to travel to Ruweished for prescreening or resettlement 
interviews.  Information regarding specific security 
arrangements at UNHCR's Amman office was reported refs b and 
c.
