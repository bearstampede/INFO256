¶1.  (C)  A/DCM on June 30 delivered ref a oral message from 
the Secretary on Jordan's aid pledges to the Palestinian 
Authority to Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant to Foreign 
Minister Marwan Muasher.  EconCouns that same day delivered 
the message to Bassam Awadallah, Minister of Planning, who 
confirmed that Jordan has only paid USD 500,000 of its Beirut 
pledge to the PA.  Both Awadallah and Ayed promised to check 
on the status of the payment of the remainder of Jordan's 
Beirut Summit pledge.
