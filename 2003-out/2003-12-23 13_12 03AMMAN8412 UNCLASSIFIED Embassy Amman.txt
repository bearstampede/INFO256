SUBJECT: BRIEFING ON LIBYA WMD PROGRAMS: JORDAN 
 
REF: SECSTATE 345870 
 
PolOff briefed Basheer Zoubi, Deputy Director of Foreign 
Minister Muasher's Private Office, December 22 on 
developments regarding Libyan WMD and missile programs. 
Zoubi was given the texts of the President's statement, the 
Fact Sheet on the President's National Security Strategy to 
Combat WMD, and background points.  Zoubi welcomed Libya's 
recent actions to end its WMD programs and thanked PolOff for 
the information.
