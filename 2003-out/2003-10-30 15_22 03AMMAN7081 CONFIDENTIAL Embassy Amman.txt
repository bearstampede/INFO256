SUBJECT: FOLLOW-UP DEMARCHE FOR U.S. CANDIDATE TO UN 
COMMITTEE AGAINST TORTURE 
 
REF: A. STATE 303519 
     ¶B. AMMAN 06741 
 
Classified By: Acting DCM Doug Silliman for Reasons 1.5 (B,D) 
 
¶1.  (U) PolOff delivered a follow-up demarche October 30 
supporting the candidacy of Ms. Felice Gaer for re-election 
to the UN Committee Against Torture to Ayman Al-Amiri, Acting 
Head of the International Organizations (IO) Department of 
the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Dr. Khaled Takhayneh, 
also of the IO Department.  Post previously approached the 
MFA on October 20 in support of Ms. Gaer's candidacy (see ref 
b). 
 
¶2.  (C) In response, Takhayneh said that Ms. Gaer was "well 
qualified" and that it was "likely" that Jordan would vote in 
favor of her re-election.
