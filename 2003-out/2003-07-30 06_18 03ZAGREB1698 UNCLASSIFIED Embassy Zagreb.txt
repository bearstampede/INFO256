SUBJECT: FORMAL NOTIFICATION OF CROATIAN INTENT TO DEPLOY 
TROOPS TO IRAQ 
 
REF: ZAGREB 1364 
 
¶1.  ON JUNE 12, THE CROATIAN GOVERNMENT PASSED A 
"DETERMINATION" TO DEPLOY AN UNSPECIFIED NUMBER OF MILITARY 
PERSONNEL TO IRAQ TO PARTICIPATE IN MULTINATIONAL 
PEACEKEEPING EFFORTS (REFTEL).  AFTER BEING INFORMED THAT 
CENTCOM REQUIRES FORMAL DIPLOMATIC NOTIFICATION, CROATIA'S 
FOREIGN MINISTRY FORWARDED A DIPLOMATIC NOTE REPORTING THE 
GOC ACTION.  THE NOTE (INFORMAL EMBASSY TRANSLATION BELOW) 
WAS DATED JULY 23 AND WAS RECEIVED AT THE EMBASSY ON JULY 29. 
 IN ADDITION TO THE INFORMATION IN THE ORIGINAL GOC 
DETERMINATION, THE NOTE SPECIFIES THAT CROATIA'S OFFER WILL 
CONSIST OF SPECIAL OPERATIONS TROOPS FOR SERVICE UNDER U.S. 
COMMAND.  THE NOTE ALSO STATES THAT A FINAL DECISION TO 
DEPLOY MUST BE APPROVED BY CROATIA'S PARLIAMENT. 
 
¶2.  BEGIN INFORMAL EMBASSY TRANSLATION: 
 
(COMPLIMENTARY OPENING)  THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF 
CROATIA, AT THE SESSION HELD ON JUNE 12, 2003, MADE A 
DETERMINATION THAT, BASED ON THE INTEREST AND READINESS OF 
THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA TO CONTRIBUTE TO AND ASSIST IN THE 
POSTWAR STABILIZATION OF IRAQ, WHICH IS IN ACCORDANCE WITH UN 
SECURITY COUNCIL RESOLUTION 1483, THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA 
AFFIRMS ITS READINESS TO CONTRIBUTE MEMBERS OF ITS ARMED 
FORCES TO THE INTERNATIONAL COALITION FORCES IN PEACE AND 
ORDER KEEPING OPERATIONS AIMED AT CREATING CONDITIONS FOR 
NORMALIZATION OF THE SITUATION, DEMOCRATIC DEVELOPMENT AND 
RECONSTRUCTION OF IRAQ. 
 
    IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE ABOVE MENTIONED DETERMINATION, THE 
GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS PREPARED TO OFFER A 
UNIT OF SPECIAL OPERATIONS TROOPS, WHICH WOULD PARTICIPATE IN 
PHASE IV OPERATIONS FOR ESTABLISHING AND KEEPING THE PEACE IN 
IRAQ, UNDER THE COMMAND OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA. 
 
THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS PREPARED, AFTER 
COORDINATION WITH APPROPRIATE BODIES OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE 
REPUBLIC OF CROATIA AND THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA, TO COME 
TO AGREEMENT ON THE MAKEUP, NUMBER AND MODALITY OF ACTIVITY 
OFTHE MILITARY PERSONNEL WHO WOULD BE INCLUDED IN THE 
INTERNATIONAL COALITION FORCES IN IRAQ. 
 
THE FINAL DECISION ON DEPLOYMENT OF MEMBERS OF THE ARMED 
FORCES ON A MISSION IN IRAQ WILL BE TAKEN BY THE CROATIAN 
SABOR (PARLIAMENT). 
 
(COMPLIMENTARY CLOSING) 
 
END INFORMAL EMBASSY TRANSLATION.
