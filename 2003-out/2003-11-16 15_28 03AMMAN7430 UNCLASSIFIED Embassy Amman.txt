SUBJECT: JORDANIAN SUPPORT FOR U.S. CANDIDATE TO UN 
COMMITTEE ON THE ELIMINATION OF ALL FORMS OF RACIAL 
DISCRIMINATION 
 
REF: STATE 309039 
 
PolOff covered reftel talking points November 12 with Dr. 
Khaled Takhayneh of the International Organizations 
Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in support of 
the candidacy of Ralph Boyd to the UN Committee on the 
Elimination of All Forms of Racial Discrimination (CERD). 
PolOff later delivered a diplomatic note in support of Mr. 
Boyd's candidacy.  On November 16, Dr. Takhayneh faxed a 
diplomatic note to PolOff stating that Jordan will vote in 
favor of the U.S. candidate to the CERD.
