will support for the UN Committee Against Torture election on 
November 26.  The MFA expects to take a final decision on 
November 20.  While not promising to support U.S. candidate 
Felice Gaer, MFA contacts report that the GoNL views her 
candidacy favorably.  Further information will be reported
