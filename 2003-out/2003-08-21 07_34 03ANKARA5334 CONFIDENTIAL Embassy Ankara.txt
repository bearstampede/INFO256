SUBJECT: 58TH UNGA ATTENDANCE:  INITIAL HIGH-LEVEL TURKISH 
RSVP 
 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 184648 
     ¶B. ANKARA 4357 
 
 
Turkish MFA UN Department Head Kaya has provided 
preliminary high-level Turkish attendance list for 58th 
UNGA.  Per ref (A) format: 
 
 
¶A.  Head of state will not attend. 
 
 
¶B.  Head of government will not attend. 
 
 
¶C.  Foreign Minister: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the 
Republic of Turkey Abdullah Gul will attend. 
 
 
Gul will arrive New York Kennedy Airport Sunday, Sept. 21, 
depart Saturday, Sept. 27.  Gul and his delegation are 
planning to arrive via THY 001 at 4:30 (1630) PM. 
 
 
Gul plans to attend UNGA General Debate and will speak 
Sept. 26. 
 
 
Gul's spouse, Mrs. Hayrunnisa Gul, will accompany him to 
New York. 
 
 
Gul will not require interpreting in bilats. 
 
 
¶D.  Attendance of other VIPs: 
 
 
--Umit Pamir, Ambassador, Turkish Permanent Representative 
to the United Nations; 
 
 
--Faruk Logoglu, Turkish Ambassador to the United States; 
 
 
--Nabi Sensoy, Ambassador, Deputy Undersecretary (General 
Political Affairs, Multilateral Political Affairs, 
International Security Affairs); 
 
 
--Prof. Dr. Ahmet Davutoglu, Ambassador (Advisor to Foreign 
Minister); 
 
 
--Ertugrul Apakan, Ambassador, General Director (Cyprus, 
Greece, Maritime, Aviation); 
 
 
--Tahsin Burcuoglu, Ambassador, General Director (Bilateral 
Political Affairs, Middle East and Africa); 
 
 
--Deniz Bolukbasi, Ambassador, Counsel (Foreign Ministry 
Advisor); 
 
 
--Ertan Tezgor, Deputy Director General (Central Asia and 
Caucasus); 
 
 
--Gurcan Turkoglu, Minister Plenipotentiary (Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs Special Advisor); 
 
 
--Huseyin Dirioz, Minister Plenipotentiary, Spokesman of 
the Ministry; 
 
 
--Tunc Ugdul, Deputy Director General (International 
Political Organizations); 
 
 
--Omer Gucuk, Head of Cabinet of the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs. 
 
 
Spouses of VIP delegation will not attend.  Turkish 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs staff will not need 
interpreters. 
 
 
Two members of parliament from the Turkish Grand National 
Assembly will attend; names to follow.  Information not 
currently available regarding attendance of M.P.'s 
spouses.  While M.P.s may need interpreters, MFA thinks the 
Turkish mission can provide interpreting services for them. 
 
 
Five security officers; names to follow at a later date. 
EDELMAN
