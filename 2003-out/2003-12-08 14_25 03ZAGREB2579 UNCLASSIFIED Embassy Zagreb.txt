SUBJECT: CROATIA SUPPORTS DELAY ON HUMAN CLONING RESOLUTION 
 
REF: A. STATE 335651 
     ¶B. ZAGREB 2367 
     ¶C. ZAGREB 2335 
 
CROATIA'S POSITION HAS NOT CHANGED ON THE HUMAN CLONING 
RESOLUTION, ACCORDING TO DUBRAVKA SIMONOVIC, MFA DEPARTMENT 
HEAD FOR HUMAN RIGHTS (SEE REF B).  SIMONOVIC, WHO JUST 
RETURNED FROM A MEETING OF THE COUNCIL OF EUROPE'S STEERING 
    COMMITTEE ON BIO-MEDICAL ETHICS, AFFIRMED FOR US ON DECEMBER 
8 THAT CROATIA MAINTAINS ITS SUPPORT FOR A DELAY OF 
CONSIDERATION OF AN INTERNATIONAL INSTRUMENT TO BAN HUMAN 
CLONING.
