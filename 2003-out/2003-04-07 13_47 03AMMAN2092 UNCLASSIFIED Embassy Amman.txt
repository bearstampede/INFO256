SUBJECT: JORDAN WILL SUPPORT U.S. CANDIDACY TO UNESCO 
 
REF: (A) STATE 54900 (B) STATE 75379 
 
1.(U) On April 2, Poloff conveyed ref a dipnote and talking 
points to the Jordanian MFA, announcing the U.S.'s intention 
to re-enter UNESCO on October 1 and seeking support for the 
U.S. candidacy to UNESCO's Executive Board.  On April 7, 
Jusuf Bataineh of the MFA's International Organizations 
department verbally informed poloff that Jordan would support 
the U.S. candidacy.  He said that a dipnote formally 
announcing this GOJ decision would be delivered shortly.
