(C) Per request reftel, Econchief called Dr. Rohan Perera, 
Legal Advisor to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to determine 
the status of the GSL's submission of the 1455 report. 
Perera expressed surprise, believing the GSL was up-to-date 
on all its reporting and cited UNSC 1373 requirements.  When 
informed that the 1455 report could be very similar to the 
1373 reporting requirement, Perera promised to look into the 
matter and determine what the GSL still needed to do.  Per 
instructions reftel, Post will not demarche the Maldives.
