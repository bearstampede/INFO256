SUBJECT:  INL:  Amendment to LOA signed with Police 
Narcotics Bureau 
 
Ref:  State 237493 
 
Per Reftel, the Ambassador and Pujith Jayasundara, 
Director of the Sri Lanka Police Narcotics Bureau (PNB), 
signed an amendment to the PNB Letter of Agreement for 
additional funding of USD 250,000 on September 16, 2003. 
The amendment provides for additional assistance to the 
PNB for the training and managerial assistance project 
sponsored by the U.S. Justice Department.  The signed 
amendment has been sent by pouch to INL/AAE.
