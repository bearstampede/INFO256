Department (CID) and Directorate of Internal Intelligence

missing aircraft.  On a related issue, NAT Aviation and 
Serendib Express owner S.S. Nathan was positively identified 
by ECON to be Amcit Sivanathan SIVARASA (DPOB 04/10/60 - Sri 
Lanka) based on DSP-11 photo for Passport No. 045541778. 
S.S. Nathan is accessible in Sri Lanka and might possibly 
agree to an interview should one be deemed appropriate.
