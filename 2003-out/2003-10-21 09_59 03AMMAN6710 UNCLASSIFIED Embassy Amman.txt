SUBJECT: REVISED - CORRECTED COUNTRY CLEARANCE REQUEST FOR 
AMMAN-BASED REGIONAL ENGLISH LANGUAGE OFFICER RELO JOHN 
TUREK AND RELO ASSISTANT SALWA RIHANI TDY IN BEIRUT - 
LEBANON NOVEMBER 2 - 7, 2003 
 
  ¶1.   Amman-based Regional English Language Officer (RELO) 
      John Turek and RELO Assistant Salwa Rihani request country 
      clearance for their upcoming TDY in Beirut November 2 - 7, 
      ¶2003. RELO Tureks' title is Attach and holds top security 
      clearance. 
 
  ¶2.   Justification for this TDY: as part of the quarterly 
      contentious cooperation between the PAS Beirut - RELO to 
      promote English Language programs with Lebanese 
      institutions, and as part of the English Teaching Programs 
      goals in the region to enhance English teaching capacity in 
      order to provide English teachers with the latest English 
      teaching methodology and pedagogy, RELO Turek will conduct 
      several meetings with the following institutions/ 
      universities: Center for Education and Research Development 
      (CERD), The Lebanese University, the Islamic University, 
      Ministry of Education English Teaching Inspectors at the 
      Directorate of Inspection, meetings with ATEL Chairman and 
      the new English Language Fellow at CERD, heads of the 
      English Departments at the Lebanese Universities, and 
      several schools to assess their English teaching curricula. 
 
  ¶3.   To reduce human resources/usage cost, some of these 
      meetings will be conducted at the Embassy keeping off- 
      compound social/representational activities to a minimum. 
 
  ¶4.   The following is RELO Turek and RELO Assistant Rihani 
      itinerary.
