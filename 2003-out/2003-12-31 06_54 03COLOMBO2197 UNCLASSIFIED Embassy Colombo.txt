SUBJECT:  Colombo Plan:  Mission concurs with revisions 
to Letter of Agreement amendment 
 
Refs:  (A) INL-Colombo 12-31-03 unclass email 
-      (B) Colombo 1990, and previous 
 
Per Ref A, Mission concurs with the revised wording for 
the amendment to the Letter of Agreement with the 
Colombo Plan.  The amendment, which provides the Colombo 
Plan Drug Advisory Program with USD 110,000, will fund 
two regional narcotics officer training courses to be 
conducted by the U.S. Department of Justice's Drug 
Enforcement Agency.
