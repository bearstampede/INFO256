(U) Classified by Political Counselor John Kunstadter for 
reasons 1.5 (b) and (d). 
 
 
(C) We delivered reftel demarche to UN Attache Orcun Baser, 
in the absence of Department Head Gokcen Kaya.  Baser 
commented informally that Turkey supports U.S. resolutions 
regarding HIV/AIDS, women's participation in the political 
process, cyber-security but is undecided on cloning and is 
currently querying medical faculties about this issue.  Baser 
said the Multilateral Economics Department is still reviewing 
issues related to Second Committee Reform and UN budget 
discipline.  Post will follow up and provide further details 
as MFA makes its decisions on these issues.
