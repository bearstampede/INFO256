SUBJECT: IFC SMALL BUSINESS FACILITY FOR IRAQ 
 
REF: SECSTATE 342324 
 
The Dutch Ministry of Finance confirmed 12/17 that the 
Netherlands would vote in favor of the proposed IFC Small 
Business Financing Facility for Iraq at the 12/18 IFC Board 
meeting, but would not make a financial contribution to it.
