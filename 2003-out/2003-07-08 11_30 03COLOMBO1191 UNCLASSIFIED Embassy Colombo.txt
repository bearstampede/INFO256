(SBU) On July 8, Mission received a dipnote from the 
Government of Sri Lanka confirming that it has completed 
the necessary domestic legal requirements for the ICC 
Artical 98 non-surrender accord's entry into force.  The 
dipnote date -- signed on July 4, 2003 -- stands as the 
accord's entry into force date.
