(C) Poloff Oct. 20 delivered reftel talking points to Kemal 
Tuzun, acting head of the MFA Middle East Department.  Tuzun 
said the GOT has not determined how it will vote on the 
resolutions concerning the Israeli security fence.  He said 
the GOT is considering the USG position, but will likely 
follow the EU's lead if an EU consensus emerges.  Tuzun noted 
that Turkey has in the past voted in favor of UNGA 
resolutions introduced by the Palestinians, while issuing a 
statement outlining the GOT position on terrorism and the 
Middle East peace process.
