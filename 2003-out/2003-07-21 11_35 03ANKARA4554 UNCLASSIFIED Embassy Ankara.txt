SUBJECT:  GOT Leaning Toward Support for U.S. Position 
on Patent Cooperation Treaty Fees 
 
 
Ref:  State 126523 
 
 
Oya Karakas, MFA Head of Department for Multilateral 
Economic Affairs, told Econoff July 18 that the GOT 
agrees in principle with the U.S. proposal to reduce 
Patent Cooperation Treaty fees (reftel), but that it 
will probably join whatever consensus emerges on this 
issue.  Pearson
