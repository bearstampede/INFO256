SUBJECT: VATICAN REVIEWING USG APPROACH TO DISARMAMENT AT 
UNGA 
 
REF: STATE 217408 
 
DCM MET AUGUST 20 WITH HOLY SEE DEPUTY FOREIGN MINISTER 
PIETRO PAROLIN AND DELIVERED THE NON-PAPER AND TALKING POINTS 
    FROM REFTEL, EMPHASIZING THE U.S. COMMITMENT TO MULTILATERAL 
ARMS CONTROL THAT WOULD ADD TO SECURITY AND STABILITY. 
PAROLIN ACKNOWLEDGED THE IMPORTANCE OF ENSURING THAT THE 
FIRST COMMITTEE FOCUS ON TODAY'S THREATS AND SAID HE WOULD 
REFER THE PAPERS TO THE HOLY SEE'S OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR 
DISARMAMENT, PAOLO CONVERSI.  POLOFF WILL FOLLOW UP WITH 
CONVERSI AND REPORT FURTHER VATICAN REACTION SEPTEL.
